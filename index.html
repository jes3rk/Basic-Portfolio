About Me

A graduate from the University of Virginia with a Bachelor's in Economics, Joseph Schefer is a forward thinking developer and designer. With a proven track record of excellent customer service and client-facing projects from his time as an audiovisual and events technician, Joseph can not only build websites, but work with the client to design them as well.


In his spare time, he is an avid musician, composer, and sound designer, with his compositions featured on the stage and screen. Exerpts from that work can be found at jschefermusic.com
